How CareConnect Works

A simple three-step journey from registration to receiving or giving care.

01
📝
Register Your Need or Skill
Patients describe their support need. Volunteers share their skills and availability. It takes under 3 minutes.
02
🤖
AI-Assisted Matching
Our system analyses requests and automatically suggests the best-matched volunteer based on location, skills, and urgency.
03
💚
Connect & Receive Care
A coordinator confirms the match and facilitates the connection. Feedback is collected to improve future matchings.
